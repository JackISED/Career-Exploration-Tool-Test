ISED has over 5000 jobs all over Canada in a variety of fields.

Explore the career possibilities you can have!

The public service is organized by classification of jobs, which groups similar jobs in the same classification, which often have similar education requirement.

Start your exploration by seeing what type of job your education program makes you eligible for.

Then learn about the types of jobs in this classification at ISED. It's important to note that some education programs are not necessarily relevant for ISED, but may be at another department, and that some educational programs may be eligible but not indicated.
