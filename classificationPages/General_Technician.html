General Technical (GT) Definition

The Technical Services Group is focused on jobs that involve doing, inspecting, and leading skilled technical tasks.
To be more specific, this group includes jobs that mainly handle:
Designing three-dimensional exhibits or displays, staying within a set budget and chosen theme.
Operating television cameras and video recording systems and equipment.
Building and fixing prostheses (artificial body parts) and orthoses (supportive devices like braces).
Performing other technical functions not listed above.
Planning, developing, and conducting training in these areas, or leading any of these activities.
However, jobs that mainly fit into another group are not included in the Technical Services Group.
