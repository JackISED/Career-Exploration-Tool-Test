Education (ED) Group Definition

The Education (ED) category of the Education and Library Science group includes jobs that are mainly about using a deep understanding of teaching methods to educate and advise students in schools, and also to teach, train, and counsel young people and adults in programs outside of school. It also involves doing research and giving advice about education.
To be more specific, this category includes jobs that mainly focus on:
Using a deep understanding of educational methods to teach or counsel students in schools or in outside programs.
Using this knowledge to create, run, or evaluate special education programs like language training, job training, adult education, teaching how to read and write, and health education.
Leading any of the activities mentioned above.
However, jobs that are mostly about other things or that focus mainly on planning and giving courses for college undergraduates and graduates are not included in this group.
