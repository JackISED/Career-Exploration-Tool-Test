Program and Administrative Services (PM) Definition

The Program and Administrative Services Group is focused on jobs that mainly involve planning, creating, implementing, or managing administrative tasks and government initiatives for the public or public service. This includes:
Developing, planning, implementing, or managing government policies and services for the public or public service.
Working on the development and management of government policies and activities related to tax collection and managing other public revenues.
Designing and implementing programs for the inspection of consumer products.
Overseeing policies and activities related to the privacy of information and access to it.
Leading any of the activities mentioned above.
