Technical Inspection (TI) Definition

The Technical Services Group is made up of jobs that mainly focus on doing, checking, and leading skilled technical work.

This group includes jobs that mostly deal with things like:

  1. Checking and judging the quality of systems, processes, equipment, products, materials, and related parts, including electronic devices used in measuring things for trade.
  2. Creating, suggesting, or enforcing laws, rules, standards, or policies for quality assurance, as well as ways to do things.
  3. This includes looking into accidents, problems, or disagreements.
  4. Writing standards, specifications, instructions, or manuals about these topics.
  5. Planning, creating, and leading training in these areas or being in charge of such activities.


What's Not Included:

  1. Jobs that don't fit into the Technical Services Group are those that are mainly part of another group.
  2. Jobs where you need to be a trained aircraft pilot with a valid pilot's license are not included.
